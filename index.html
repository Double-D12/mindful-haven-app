Desk Stretches

Neck Rolls
Slowly tilt your head to the right, bringing your ear toward your shoulder. Hold for 15 seconds, then slowly roll your chin to your chest and over to the left side. Repeat 3 times on each side.
15
Shoulder Rolls
Sit up straight and slowly roll your shoulders up toward your ears, then back and down in a circular motion. Repeat 5 times forward and 5 times backward. Keep your movements slow and controlled.
30
Wrist Stretch
Extend your right arm with palm facing down. Gently pull your fingers back toward your body with your left hand. Hold for 15 seconds, then switch arms. Repeat 2 times for each arm.
15
Seated Spinal Twist
Sit up straight and place your right hand on your left knee. Gently twist your torso to the left, using your chair for support. Hold for 20 seconds, then switch sides. Repeat 2 times for each side.
20
